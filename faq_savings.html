6. How do I change Bank Account details?

In order to change your bank account details, simply go to the Settings tab on the GoldLane app, click on Bank Details and press Edit.

Unfortunately, once an order is placed, you will not be able to cancel it.

8. What is the minimum sell value?

The Minimum selling value for digital gold and silver must be greater than or equal to Rs. 10.

9. In case of instant transfers, what are the charges?

While normal settlements are free of cost, we levy a charge of Rs.10/per transaction for instant settlements. However, your bank account needs to be verified for all the processes.
